Hi! I’m Ryan. I am a former postdoctoral researcher at the Santa Cruz Institute for Particle Physics (SCIPP), who worked with the ATLAS experiment at CERN.

I have experience in deep learning, large-scale language modeling, machine learning, pattern recognition, statistical modeling, data science, and petabyte data reduction. I am passionate about using scientific techniques to solve important problems and about how technologies extend our reach.
