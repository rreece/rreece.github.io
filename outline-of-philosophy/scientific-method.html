The Scientific Method

This outline discusses the definition of the scientific method, its central role in founding a scientific epistemology, demarcating science from pseudoscience, and other epistemological topics concerning the methods and philosophy of science. The metaphysical implications of science are discussed in the outline on scientific realism.
