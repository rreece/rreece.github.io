Constructive Empiricism

Van Fraassen.

Science aims to give us theories that are empiriccally adequate, but does not justify metaphysical claims about reality.

Structural Realism

Poincaré, Worrall, Ladyman, Psillos.

Science has identified real patterns, relationships, and structures (at least within a regime) in nature.
