October Fast Report

October 10, 2018

Last weekend I did my first 48-hour fast.

I have been doing intermittent fasting all summer. It has some real health advantages, including XXX, XXX, and XXX,1 but that is a different story than I am reporting here. Still, intermittent fasting made me want to learn more about the health effects of fasting in general and interested in trying a prolonged fast of 48-72 hours.

Why?

I have a few reasons why I am interested in prolonged fasting.

  1. To reset

    This year I started a new job, one where there is an exceptional amount of free food available.

    We eat too often today.

  2. To detox

    Cutting alcohol for the month of October (#SoberOctober). It has great effects.2

    Not eating gives your body time to do other things.

  3. To learn about my body

    The hierarchy of energy sources.

    Ketones as the 4th energy macronutrient.
